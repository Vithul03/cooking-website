The Origin of the name Kitchen Windows

The name "Kitchen Windows" refers to the surf spot to the right of the restaurant. Jeffrey's Bay has always been one of the best fishing areas in South Africa. The fishermen used to refer to the area now called Kitchen Windows as Little Reef. It became a popular surf spot during the 60's and the spot became known as Kitchen Windows as the surfers of the time looked through their kitchen window to see if the surf was firing.
